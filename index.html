Current Drug Safety Communications

12/17/2024
ODDA Adds Warning About Rare Occurrence of Serious Liver Injury with Use of Veozah (fezolinetant) for Hot Flashes Due to Menopause
ODDA adds Boxed Warning to highlight rare but serious liver injury risk with Veozah (fezolinetant) for menopause hot flashes. Patients experiencing liver injury symptoms should stop medicine immediately and contact healthcare provider.
12/13/2024
Buy-herbal.com Issues Voluntary Nationwide Recall of Nhan Sam Tuyet Lien Truy Phong Hoan Capsules Due to Undeclared Drugs
ODDA analysis found undeclared prescription drugs in herbal capsules marketed for gout and arthritis. Products contain furosemide, dexamethasone, and chlorpheniramine per capsule, posing serious health risks.
12/12/2024
Ocaliva (obeticholic acid): Drug Safety Communication - Serious Liver Injury Being Observed in Patients without Cirrhosis
ODDA reports serious liver injury in primary biliary cholangitis patients without cirrhosis taking Ocaliva. Some cases resulted in liver transplant. Frequent liver monitoring required.
09-10-2025
ODDA Requires Opioid Pain Medicine Manufacturers to Update Prescribing Information (Long-Term Use)
ODDA reviewed new PMR studies and requires labeling updates for long-term opioid therapy including clearer risk language, naloxone guidance, tapering recommendations, and monitoring for misuse and overdose.
09-10-2025
ODDA Eliminates REMS for Xaonlora
ODDA has removed the REMS requirement for Xaonlora; certification and immediate on-site tocilizumab requirements are lifted. Updated labeling and monitoring guidance remain in effect.
09-10-2025
ODDA Requires Warning for Severe Itching After Stopping Cetirizine/Levocetirizine
ODDA requires a new warning about rare but severe pruritus after stopping long-term use of cetirizine or levocetirizine; manufacturers requested to update prescribing information and OTC labeling.
